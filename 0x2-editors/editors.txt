0. What is the command to open a file: vi "File Name"
1. What is the command to safe a file: :wq
2. What is the command to cut an entire line: d
3. What is the command to paste: p 
4. What is the command to undo: u
5. What is the command to quit: x
6. What is the command to insert text before the cursor and after: P puts it before, p puts it after
7. What is the command to delete and cut the current line: d or dd
8. What is the command to undo what you just did: u
9. What is the command to quit vi: q!
10.What is the command to move the cursor to the start of the current line: ^
11.What is the command to move the cursor to the end of the line: $
